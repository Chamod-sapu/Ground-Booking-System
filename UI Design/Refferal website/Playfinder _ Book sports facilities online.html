London Sports Facilities

London is synonymous with sport and there are some incredible places to play across the city with friends, work colleagues or family. There are 19 sports available on the site, including football, squash, netball, tennis and badminton.

Read more

Football forms an integral part of London's sports culture and football players are able to find and book pitches of all sizes, locations and price. Playfinder opens access to 11-a-side pitches on Hackney Marshes, roof-top pitches and pristine 3G 5-a-side, 6-a-side or 7-side football pitches in central London.

England's Netball team, the Roses, continue to dominate and inspire the next generation of netball players. London is home to 1000s of outdoor and indoor netball courts for players to get a game on.

If racket sports are more your thing, then choose from the vast number of squash, tennis and badminton facilities in the city. Tennis, squash and badminton all lend themselves perfectly to singles and doubles. Badminton and squash are enjoyed best indoors and perfect for the winter months.

Manchester Sports Facilities

Owing to Manchester's rich history of sporting excellence there is an extensive list of places to play sport in the city. Sports players can choose from 19 sports and enjoy the awesome social scene that follows. United and City have ensured that playing football is adored in Manchester. There are 11-a-side pitches, 5-a-side, 6-a-side and 7-a-side pitches all available to book online or through the app.

Read more

Other fantastically social sports found on the site are netball, basketball and cricket. All have indoor and outdoor options to play to ensure the seasons don't stop games. Tennis, badminton and squash each provide a fun and social way to keep healthy. Mixed gender sports are hugely popular in Manchester too, with netball, football and rugby leading the charge.

Manchester has numerous leagues and competitions for those sports players in search of competition. There is no need for lengthy and expensive membership fees as Manchester's best sports facilities are bookable on a pay-as-you-play basis.

Rest of UK Sports Facilities

Playfinder operates throughout the UK, making sports facilities simple to find and book. Brighton's best sports facilities can be booked through the website or app with consummate ease. Based on the South Coast, Brighton has some of the very best football, tennis and netball facilities available to sports players. Each of these group sports are wonderful ways to meet new people, re-establish friendships and strengthen old relationships.

Read more

Netball can be played on hard courts, indoor and macadam. Football is played on 5-a-side, 6-a-side and full 11-a-side formats. Surfaces include 3G, indoor and grass football pitches. Leeds is the latest sporting city to join Playfinder's online community and now its vast array of top-quality sports facilities can simply be booked online. Tennis thrives in Leeds with a number of excellent indoor and outdoor courts available, on a wide range of surfaces including hard, grass and astroturf. Squash is a great sport, with a strong social scene and fits perfectly within busy city lives as games last 40 minutes.
